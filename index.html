About Me

Victoria Pereira is a Texas based singer-songwriter. She is currently a sophmore at Wesleyan University in Middletown, Connecticuit and majoring in Music and Computer Science. In her early stages, Victoria used to sit and write songs in her bedroom closet as a way to pass the time. Now, she enjoys preforming and recording her music to share her heartelf lyrics that expose the stinging realities of life. Her other hobbies include reading and rowing, and sheis a collegete athlete rowing for the Wesleyan Women's crew team.
